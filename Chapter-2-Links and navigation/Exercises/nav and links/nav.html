Movie: Elemental

Summary

Elemental is a new animated rom-com drama film by Disney Pixar that does set out with the same innocent touch one would expect of it but ends up saying much more. The film journeys alongside an unlikely pair, Ember and Wade, in a city where fire-, water-, land- and air-residents live together. Despite being warm and colorful in its visuals and seeming like a kids’ film at first glance, Elemental does talk about real-world issues and struggles that many youngsters grow up around in this modern day.
